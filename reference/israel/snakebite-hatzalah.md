## Inferred Protocol (based on alignment with MDA/national standards)

Since United Hatzalah EMTs are trained to the same national standards as MDA, the following protocol points are expected to apply:

1. Call for emergency help (United Hatzalah: **1221**, or MDA: **101**)
2. Keep victim calm, lying down, with bitten limb immobilised below heart level
3. Remove jewellery and constrictive clothing from bite area
4. Wash bite with soap and water only (no alcohol-based disinfectant)
5. Photograph snake from safe distance if possible
6. Do NOT apply tourniquet, suck venom, cut wound, cool area, or give food/drink
7. Transport to hospital for evaluation and possible antivenom

**Important**: This inferred protocol should be verified against United Hatzalah's actual training materials if/when they become available.
