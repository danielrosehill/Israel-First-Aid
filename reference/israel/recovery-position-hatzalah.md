## GAP FLAG

**No specific recovery position protocol was found published on United Hatzalah's website (1221.org.il) or on Hatzalah Without Borders (hatzalah.org.il).**

### What Was Searched
- United Hatzalah (1221.org.il) — their basic first aid course covers CPR (adult and infant), bleeding control, and fracture stabilisation. Recovery position is likely taught within the CPR module but is not separately documented on the public site.
- Hatzalah Without Borders (hatzalah.org.il) — their life-saving guides do not include a standalone recovery position guide.

### What Is Known

United Hatzalah's free basic first aid course (offered as a public service) covers:
- Adult and infant CPR/resuscitation
- Wound management including haemorrhage control
- Fracture stabilisation

The recovery position would be taught as part of the CPR/resuscitation module (as a step for unconscious breathing casualties), but the specific technique is not documented separately in their publicly available materials.

United Hatzalah's online learning platform (school.1221.org.il) hosts course content that may include recovery position instruction, but this requires enrollment to access.

### Recommendation

For Israeli recovery position protocols, rely on MDA guidelines. See recovery-position-mda.md for the detailed protocol. United Hatzalah's training aligns with Israeli Ministry of Health standards.
