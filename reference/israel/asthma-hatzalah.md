### What Is Known

United Hatzalah's volunteer first responders carry medic bags that include equipment for respiratory emergencies. Their EMT training (NREMT-level) covers asthma management as part of the standard curriculum, including:
- Assessment of respiratory distress
- Assisting with patient's own prescribed inhaler
- Oxygen administration
- Recognition of when advanced care is needed

United Hatzalah's operational response to asthma calls typically involves:
- Rapid dispatch of nearest volunteer (average 3-minute response time)
- On-scene stabilisation
- Coordination with arriving MDA ambulance for transport

### Recommendation

For Israeli asthma first aid protocols, rely on MDA guidelines and the Israeli Asthma Association (asthma.org.il). See asthma-mda.md for the detailed protocol.
