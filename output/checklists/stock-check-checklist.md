## Schedule

Check your first aid kit **twice per year** using these natural calendar markers:

| Check | Timing | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Check 1 | **Before Pesach** (March/April) | Pre-summer prep. Also coincides with spring cleaning — check for used/depleted items |
| Check 2 | **Before Rosh Hashana** (September/October) | Post-summer review. Heat damage assessment. Pre-winter prep |

**Vehicle kits**: Check every **3-4 months** due to accelerated heat degradation in Israeli summers (35-45C in car).

## Heat Damage Warning (Israel-Specific)

Israeli summers accelerate degradation of:
- **Adhesives** (plasters, tape, chest seals) — adhesive melts and loses grip
- **Gels** (burn gel, eye wash) — may dry out or separate
- **Medications** — reduced efficacy, especially liquid formulations
- **EpiPens** — adrenaline degrades faster above 25C
- **Nitrile gloves** — become brittle

**Vehicle kits** are most at risk. On a 35C day, a car interior can exceed 60C. Consider:
- Insulated pouch for medication storage in vehicles
- More frequent checks (every 3 months) for vehicle kits
- Keeping backup medications indoors, not in the car
